URNEA Large Biodegradable Urn

A meaningful and sustainable way to honor your loved one, designed with care for both the environment and your memories. Crafted from vegetable-based composite, this eco-friendly urn naturally decomposes without leaving a harmful trace, making it the perfect choice for conscious and compassionate memorial solutions.

Beautiful and Versatile Design

This elegant biodegradable urn is ideal for home display, burial, or placement in a columbarium. Its refined aesthetics make it a dignified tribute that complements any memorial space, whether in a living room, garden, or sacred site.

Built for Travel and Safety

With its TSA-approved certification, this urn is safe to transport and handle through airport security. You can carry your loved one's ashes with confidence, knowing they will be protected during travel and properly stored for whenever you're ready to display or inter them.

Thoughtful and Functional

This urn is designed to hold up to 270 lbs of live weight and 274 cubic inches of ashes, making it a practical and durable option for families in need. Included with the urn are cotton and bio bags for safe handling and environmentally responsible storage.

Eco-Conscious and European Crafted

Combining modern style with European craftsmanship, URNEA offers a unique blend of quality and sustainability. Every detail is thoughtfully designed to ensure a lasting, beautiful memorial that reflects your values and respects your loved one's legacy.
