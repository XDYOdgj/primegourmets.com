Custom Cremation Urn with Purple Finish and Silver Cross

This custom cremation urn is a touching tribute to your loved one, blending elegance with enduring quality. Made from premium aluminum, it features a radiant purple finish and a high-gloss lacquer that enhances its visual appeal while providing long-term durability. The striking silver rim adds a refined touch to any display, whether it's in your home, a church sanctuary, or a columbarium.

Each urn is adorned with a personalized engraving of a silver cross, symbolizing eternal peace and spiritual devotion. This makes it a meaningful keepsake that honors the life and beliefs of the departed. The 200 cubic inch capacity is designed to accommodate the remains of an adult, and the secure threaded lid with a 3.6 inch opening ensures safe and dignified storage.

With a felt-lined base and moisture-resistant interior, this urn is built to preserve surfaces and last for years. The Splendore Series is handcrafted in Florida, USA, and crafted with care to serve as a heartfelt reminder of the love that remains. Whether you're looking for a spiritual memorial urn or a lasting tribute to someone special, this handcrafted cremation vessel is an ideal choice.

Key Features

  • Premium aluminum construction ensures long-lasting durability
  • 200 cubic inch capacity is perfect for adult remains
  • Personalized engraving with silver cross adds spiritual meaning
  • Secure threaded lid with felt-lined base for safe storage
