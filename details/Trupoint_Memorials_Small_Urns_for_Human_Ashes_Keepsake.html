Elegant & Functional Small Urns for Human Ashes

A thoughtful way to honor your loved one's memory with grace and care. Designed for both display and remembrance, these keepsake urns are perfect for creating a lasting tribute in your home or as a meaningful gift for someone special.

Durable & Beautifully Crafted

Each small urn is handcrafted from high-grade aluminum, ensuring durability that lasts for years. The exquisite white finish with silver detailing adds a touch of timeless elegance, making it a beautiful addition to any room and a heartfelt keepsake for your loved one's memory.

Thoughtful Design & Practical Features

With a secure screw-top lid, these urns provide peace of mind, protecting the ashes even if the urn is moved or handled. They also come with a velvet bag for safekeeping and a black box with funnel to simplify the process of transferring ashes, offering both comfort and practicality for your journey through grief and remembrance.
