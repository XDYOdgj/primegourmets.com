Elegant Cremation Urn with Coastal Gray Finish

This beautifully crafted cremation urn is designed to honor the memory of a loved one or a cherished pet with dignity and grace. Its refined coastal gray wooden finish adds a subtle yet striking aesthetic, making it a perfect centerpiece for any home or memorial space.

Durable & Weather-Resistant Construction

This urn features a solid MDF wrapped box that ensures long-lasting durability and resilience. The matte lacquer finish protects against scratches and wear, while maintaining a timeless look for both indoor and outdoor display.

Secure & Stable Design

With a secure bottom panel reinforced by four screws, this urn offers added stability and peace of mind. Its sturdy build makes it ideal for displaying in any setting, whether in a living room, garden, or sacred space.

Ample Interior Capacity

Offering 213 cubic inches of internal space, this urn comfortably accommodates the ashes of an adult or pet. The generous capacity ensures all your memories fit inside, with enough room to preserve them for years to come.

Custom engraving options allow you to add personalized names or meaningful messages that reflect your heartfelt memories. This makes the urn not just a vessel, but a meaningful tribute tailored to your loved one's legacy.

Whether you're looking for a decorative urn for memorials or a personalized urn to express your emotions, this elegantly designed coastal gray cremation box offers the perfect blend of beauty and practicality. It's an affordable yet high-quality choice for those who value both style and substance in their tribute.
