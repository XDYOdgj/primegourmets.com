Lily at Peace - Biodegradable Urn for a Natural Farewell

A thoughtful and beautiful biodegradabe urn by Earth Memorials, designed to honor your loved one with a serene and eco-conscious tribute. Whether you're scattering ashes at sea, releasing them in a lake, or choosing a ground burial, this urn offers a meaningful and sustainable alternative to traditional options.

This elegant biodegradable urn features a 22-inch diameter and a generous 200 cubic inch capacity, making it ideal for holding all of a person's cremated remains. Its unique design is adorned with 9 intricately detailed leaves, symbolizing growth, memory, and peace. Not only is it visually captivating, but it also serves as a reminder of the enduring connection between life and the natural world.

Constructed from sustainable materials like clay and cotton, this urn will remain intact for years until it is placed in water, where it will float for 1 to 5 minutes before sinking and naturally decomposing. This ensures your loved one's ashes are protected during transport and will return to the Earth without leaving a trace. A certificate of biodegradability is also included, making it suitable for release on cruise ships or other water-based services.
